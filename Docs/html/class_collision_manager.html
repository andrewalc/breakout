void CollisionManager::resolveBallBrick ( Ball &  b,
Brick &  br 
)

Figure out what side of Brick br the Ball b hit and change b's velocity accordingly.

Takes the difference of the position vectors for the ball and the brick. This creates a vector from the center of the brick. Dot this vector with each of the cardinal directions and keep the one with the highest dot product, this tells you which side the ball landed on.
